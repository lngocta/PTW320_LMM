LMM Tuition Assistance Program

LMM’s tuition assistance program complies with the IRC Tuition Reimbursement program outlined in Section 127. For more information on the IRC’s Education Assistance Program, click here.

Effective January 24, 2024

  • Participants in the program are not eligible to receive payment for the same program or course more than one time.
  • Failure to provide accurate information or not enroll in a course for which financial assistance has been requested will be considered a violation of the Company's Code of Conduct subject to disciplinary action up to and including termination.
  • In the United States, any tuition assistance over $5,250 per year is considered taxable income. Any tax liability resulting from tuition assistance is the sole responsibility of the employee receiving the assistance. If outside of the U.S., check with local Human Resources or payroll.
  • Payments for eligible expenses will be provided through standard payroll processes as soon as administratively possible upon submission of required documentation provided participation in the program has been fully approved.

I certify that I have NOT previously received tuition support from the Company for the program or course covered by this payment request or for a different course intended to meet the same degree or certification requirement.
