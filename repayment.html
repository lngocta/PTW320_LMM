Repayment Policy

In exchange for this educational assistance payment:

  • If the employee resigns from the Company or if the employee is terminated for cause (as determined by the Company, in its sole discretion), the employee must repay 100% of any tuition assistance received within the last 18 months.
  • The employee must authorize, where legally permitted, the Company to offset against any monies, such as, but not limited to, wages, bonuses, commissions, stock incentive compensation or accrued unused vacation which are owed at the time of departure, to satisfy the employee’s assistance obligations. If a balance remains, the offset does not extinguish the remaining amount owed by the employee.
  • If the company terminates the employee for any reason other than cause, repayment of tuition assistance by the employee to the Company will be waived and the employee will be paid for all approved courses in which they are enrolled at the time of notification with approved payment request. The company will not reimburse any courses enrolled in after the notification of termination.

The employee understands and agrees to the commitments and assistance obligations stated above. The employee authorizes, to the extent allowed by law, the Company to offset against any monies, such as, but not limited to, wages, bonuses, commissions, stock incentive compensation or accrued unused vacation which are owed at the time of departure to satisfy the employee’s obligation to repay tuition assistance. If a balance remains, the offset does not extinguish the remaining amount owed by the employee or former employee.
